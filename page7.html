Would you like to learn more about music production? Here are some links to some of the people who have taught me. They are all YouTube channels and they create content related to music and music production.

In The Mix
This channel is one of my favorites. They do their music exclusively in FL Studio and have some great tutorials on topics from specific concepts and plugins to song structure in general. I highly recommend this channel.
SynthHacker
This channel is a bit more niche. There are many tutorials available for a popular plugin called Serum, which is a virtual synth that has many customizable options. The channel has guides on how to create the exact sounds from popular songs, mostly within the electronic music genre.
Adam Neely
I enjoy this channel for more general music related topics. Adam is a trained jazz musician and does video essays on musical concepts, as well as question and answer videos through his social media accounts. This channel is more broadly music related, very good for music theory related information, but I have gotten some ideas for songs from some of the content in his videos.
